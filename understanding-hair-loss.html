Understanding Hair Loss

1. What is Hair Loss?

Hair loss refers to reduced hair on the scalp. It occurs when the growth cycle is imbalanced. Excess hairfall can be explained by the Hair Growth Cycle phases: Anagen (Growth), Catagen (Transition), and Telogen (Resting).

2. What Causes Hair Fall?

Hairfall can be caused by internal and external factors including lack of nutrition, stress, pollution, and hormonal changes. Improving blood circulation to the scalp is key to recovery.

3. Male & Female Pattern Baldness

Androgenic Alopecia is often genetic but can be triggered by lifestyle and diet. Early onset can be managed with consistent Ayurvedic care.

4. Telogen Effluvium (TE)

This is temporary hair thinning often caused by stress or a major health event. Our oils are designed to nourish the scalp back into the growth phase.
